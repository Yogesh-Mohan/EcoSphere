# EcoSphere Demo Script

This script will be finalized after the functional phases are implemented and verified with demo data.

## Production Walkthrough Target

1. Open the organization ESG dashboard.
2. Review overall, environmental, social, and governance scores.
3. Drill down from an ESG score to calculation inputs.
4. Review generated carbon transactions from operational sources.
5. Inspect Scope 1, Scope 2, and Scope 3 analytics.
6. Submit CSR evidence as an employee.
7. Review and approve evidence as an ESG officer.
8. Award XP through the immutable ledger.
9. Complete a sustainability challenge.
10. Auto-award a badge from structured unlock rules.
11. Redeem a reward with point and stock validation.
12. Acknowledge a governance policy.
13. Review an overdue compliance issue.
14. Recalculate a department ESG score.
15. Export the ESG summary report.
